Pechay with Tokwa

Description

This is the easiest stir fry vegetable recipe to make. You first deep fry the tofu or tokwa, as we call it in the Philippines.

Ingredients

  • 0.5 cup Vegetable oil
  • 0.25kg Tofu
  • 4 cloves Garlic
  • 1 pc Onion
  • 1 tsp Ginger
  • 0.5 cup Water
  • Seasoning
  • 0.25 Pechay
  • 30g Oyster Sauce
